How is GHA Token defined?
1. The GHA team regards GHA Token as a payment tool, with the purpose of being able to use it in a transaction when a trading platform is built in the future.
2. Total burned of 25%, GHA and Organization locked 30%, and Defi 13%, there will be a total of 3.2 billion GHA in circulation on the market.
3. GHA company plans to use 20% of its profits every year to repurchase back those GHA Tokens that is circulating in the market and maintain a total of only 20% for market circulation! At that time, it will become a payment tool and proof of equity.
4. Once there’s only 20% balance of GHA token circulating in the market, the repurchase will ceased, and then through the blockchain technology, 20% of the profit will be distributed to the holders of GHA, this is GHA definition of equity.
