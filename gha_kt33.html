How to claim an airdrop？

1. Open the TokenPocket (TP) wallet or TRONLINK Wallet, IMTOKEN and other Tron Wallet apps.

2. Use Your Wallet Browser and Dapp to enter the URL dapp.ghatoken.com

3. Go to the drop page.

4. Click to receive the airdrop and confirm your wallet authorization to Receive 100 GHA.

5. After receiving a successful airdrop, click on the promotion reward to copy the link, share the promotion to other users, and get an additional 800GHA airdrop reward per person.

6. The GHA has chosen to use smart contracts to avoid the risk of people using robots to create thousands of wallet accounts for airdrops, which require you to pay extra for the Gas, about 5-15 Trx (about 0.5-1USDT) , please note whether there is enough Trx in your wallet at the time of the drop to pay for GAS
